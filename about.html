About us

🌼 Flowers for everyone

We are a family run business that specialises in flowers. We have a wide range of flowers to choose from and we can deliver to your home or office.

We started our business in the early 2000s and have been in the business ever since. We have been in the business for over 10 years and have been able to grow our business to the point where we can offer a wide range of flowers to our customers.

We are located in the heart of the city and we are open Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm.

We are also open for private parties and weddings.
